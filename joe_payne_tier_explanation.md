## Commission Tier Structure

The AMFG commission model uses a three-tier system based on performance across three key metrics. All three metrics must meet the threshold simultaneously to qualify for a tier.

| Metric | Bronze | Silver | Gold |
|--------|--------|--------|------|
| **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)** | $15,000+ | $20,000+ | $25,000+ |
| **Demos per Week** | 2+ | 3+ | 4+ |
| **Dials per Week** | 100+ | 100+ | 200+ |
| **Commission Rate** | 13% | 16% | 19% |

**Note:** New joiners receive a 6-month grace period where their ARR is assumed at $25,000/month for tier calculation purposes, even if actual revenue is lower. This recognizes the ramp-up period required to build a sustainable pipeline.
